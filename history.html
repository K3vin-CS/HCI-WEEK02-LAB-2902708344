History of Jakarta

Jakarta was formerly known as Batavia during the Dutch colonial period. The city has a long history as a trading port and has been influenced by many cultures.

Important Events

  1. 1527 - The city was named Jayakarta.
  2. 1619 - Dutch East India Company established Batavia.
  3. 1949 - Officially became Jakarta after Indonesian independence.
