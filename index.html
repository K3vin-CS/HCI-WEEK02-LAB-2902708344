About Jakarta

Jakarta is the capital and largest city of Indonesia, located on the northwest coast of Java. It is the economic, cultural, and political center of the country.

With its rich history and diversity, Jakarta offers many landmarks, cultural experiences, and a dynamic urban life.

Quick Facts

  • Population: over 10 million in the city proper
  • Located on Java Island
  • Time zone: WIB (UTC +7)
  • Languages: Bahasa Indonesia, many local dialects
